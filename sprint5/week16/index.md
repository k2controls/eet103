### Assignments
- Final Exam Review - You may complete multiple times. Your highest score is recorded.
- Final Exam - 30 questions consisting of multiple choice and fill-in. A formula sheet is allowed and a calulator is required. The exam must be completed during Session 2. No late testing is accepted.
- Final Project Build - The final project including the final build must be completed by Session 2. A final video of the project is produced and submitted to the course link by the end of the day on Wednesday.
