Components for final build

- Cup warmer

Steps
1. Complete final controller board checks including continuity check across output terminals when controller is activated.
2. Zip tie AC plug on cup warmer.
4. Remove rotary switch from cup warmer
5. Insert AC wire from initial switch circuit into controller terminals.
6. Insert AC wire into cable tray and controller board into housing.
7. Insert buttons into acrylic cover and secure with tape on the top. 
8. Align acrylic pushbuttons with controller board switches as you position cover. Test functionality.
9. Secure top using 4 screws.
10. Complete final instructor check. 
11. Remove zip tie from plug. Insert plug into AC socket and test warmer functionality.
12. Produce final video.
    - What did you learn?
    - What would you do differently next time?
    - Any ideas for improving design?
